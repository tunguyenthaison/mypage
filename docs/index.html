Son Tu

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor at The Mathematics Department at Michigan State University working with Olga Turanova. Before that I was a student of Hung V. Tran at University of Wisconsin - Madison.

My dissertation is about Partial Differential Equations, particularly questions related to Hamilton-Jacobi equations and optimal control theory. I am interested in applied probability, statistics, machine learning and in using these skill sets to solve real world problems.
