• Rate of convergence for periodic homogenization of convex Hamilton-Jacobi equations in one dimension,
    Asymptotic Analysis 121, 2 (2021), 171–194   ·   arxiv   ·     ·  

    Let $`u^\varepsilon`$ and $`u`$ be viscosity solutions of the oscillatory Hamilton–Jacobi equation and its corresponding effective equation. Given bounded, Lipschitz initial data, we present a simple proof to obtain the optimal rate of convergence $`\mathcal{O}(\varepsilon)`$ of $`u^\varepsilon \to u`$ as $`\varepsilon \to 0^+`$.
